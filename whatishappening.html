CURRENT EVENTS

In July 2019, ICE raids across the country began, detaining more than 2000 immigrant families. Many children were separated from their parents. According to authorities, many children will not be able to be reunited with their families for some time.
Early in the year, it became known that thousands of immigrant families had been detained at the border. Thousands of children had been separated from their parents. The conditions found at the camps are described as inhumane and unlivable. Many expressed outrage, after authorities revealed that the detained children had to fend for themselves in court. Many of these children are between 3 and 12 years old, and do not speak english.
On August 3rd, a shooting in El Paso, Texas occured. The shooter had the intention of targeting the hispanic community, especially those who are undocumented.
