We are a group a three Latina girls. We are inspired by the current events occuring in the country concerning immigration. Our goal is to create awareness about this problem. Mariana Rodriguez' parents immigrated into the USA 17 years ago, from Mexico City, Mexico. Maria Andreina came to the USA with her family two and a half years ago, from Guanare, Venezuela. Lina Urrego came to the USA with her family 9 years ago from Bogota, Colombia.
